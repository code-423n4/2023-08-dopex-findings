# [01] Place commonly used strings in constants
There are a lot of magic strings used in the entire protocol. Consider keeping them into constants and replacing all their occurrences.
Despite the fact this is not real vulnerability, by avoiding the usage of magic strings codebase will become more readable and less prone to errors.
